# Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview

The Sales Performance Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed to analyze sales trends, revenue performance, product profitability, and customer purchasing behavior. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions to improve overall business performance.

## Business Problem

Organizations generate large volumes of sales data every day, making it difficult to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, and areas requiring improvement. This dashboard was created to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support strategic decision-making.

## Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Track revenue and profit trends.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Analyze customer purchasing patterns.
* Evaluate regional sales performance.
* Support data-driven business decisions.

The dataset contains sales transaction records, including:

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Average Sales Value

### Sales Analysis

* Monthly Sales Trends
* Revenue Growth Analysis
* Sales Performance by Region

### Product Analysis

* Top Selling Products
* Category-wise Sales Performance
* Product Profitability Analysis

### Customer Analysis

* Customer Purchase Behavior
* High-Value Customers
* Customer Contribution to Revenue

### Regional Analysis

* Sales by Region
* Profit by Region
* Regional Performance Comparison

## Key Insights

* Identified the highest revenue-generating products and categories.
* Observed seasonal trends affecting sales performance.
* Highlighted regions contributing the most to overall revenue.
* Analyzed customer segments responsible for major sales contributions.
* Detected underperforming products requiring business attention.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase marketing efforts for high-performing products.
* Optimize inventory management based on demand trends.
* Focus on improving sales in underperforming regions.
* Develop customer retention strategies for high-value customers.
* Monitor product profitability regularly to improve margins.
